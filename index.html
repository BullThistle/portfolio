Rafael's Bio

Rafael is a Portland State University computer science student turned Epicurus attendee. He has always loved technology and has expressed his interest through multiple outlets. He spent a year working with the non profit Free Geek where he repurposed old computer parts in to functioning computers for schools. He has also been using various technologies to music and music software with for the past decade.
